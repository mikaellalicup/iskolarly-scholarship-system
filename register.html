Privacy Policy

1

When you sign up, we collect your name, birthday, contact number, email, address, student ID, course, year level, GPA, family background, and income details. We also collect documents you upload and basic session info like your device and browser.

2

We use your information to process your scholarship application, verify eligibility, send you updates and reminders, and let authorized officers review your documents.

3

Only you and authorized Iskolarly admins can see your data. We don't sell it or share it with anyone outside the platform, except when the law requires it.

4

Your password is encrypted and never stored as plain text. We take reasonable steps to keep your account and data secure. Keep your login credentials private.

5

You have the right to view, correct, or request deletion of your data under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173). Contact us through the platform to do so.

6

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of Iskolarly means you accept any changes.
