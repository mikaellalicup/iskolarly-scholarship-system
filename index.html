Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about applying through Iskolarly.

Who is eligible to apply for a scholarship?

+
Eligibility varies per scholarship program. Generally, applicants must be currently enrolled students in good academic standing. Check each scholarship's specific criteria after logging in.

What documents are required for application?

+
Typical requirements include: certified true copy of grades, proof of enrollment, proof of household income (if need-based), government-issued ID, and a personal essay. Additional documents may be required per program.

How long does the application process take?

+
After submission, the review process typically takes 5–10 business days. You will receive a notification through the portal and your registered email once a decision has been made.

Can I apply for multiple scholarships?

+
Yes, you may apply to multiple programs as long as you meet the individual eligibility criteria. However, note that some scholarships may restrict concurrent grants — always read the program guidelines carefully.

How do I renew my scholarship?

+
Current scholars can submit renewal requirements through their student dashboard. Log in, go to "Renewal & Compliance," upload updated documents (grades, enrollment certificate), and submit before the term deadline.
