Renewal Guidelines

GPA Requirement

Maintain a GPA of 1.75 or better. Falling below this threshold may result in probation or termination.

Unit Load

Scholars must maintain a minimum of 15 academic units per semester, unless medically excused.

No Failing Grades

Receiving an INC, W, or failing grade in any subject may affect scholarship renewal eligibility.

Deadline Compliance

Late submissions will not be accepted. Scholarship will be put on hold until complete requirements are received.

Community Service

Active scholars are encouraged to complete at least 8 hours of community service per semester.
